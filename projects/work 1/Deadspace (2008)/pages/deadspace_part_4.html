Gaining back his senses,

he looked beneath his feet.

A monstrous limb,

a human head,

and a pool of blood.

The mechanical doors cut off parts of the creature.

Might’ve been a trick of the light or his mind,

But the thing seemed to have moved ever so slightly.

Not taking any more chances, he stepped into the room.

Where the muffled cries of a man can be heard.

First– look for anything he could use to defend himself.

His eyes darted frantically in search of a weapon.

That’s when he caught a glimpse of it, screened by pipes,

After taking a short flight of stairs to the platform:

A gruesome scene enough to fuel nightmares.

In a quaint workshop decorated with power tools,

Sits a mutilated man holding onto his spilled intestines.

A victim of the creatures, certain about his death.

With bloodied hands, he approached the board

and wrote:

CUT OFF THEIR LIMBS

Far from his calm demeanor,

he thought of ways to kill,

Isaac picks up one of the mining tools atop the workstation.

The Plasma Cutter

capable of shooting ionized plasma in a straight line.

If it can cut through stone and minerals,

it should do well on the creature.

He can now aid the helpless man trapped in the next room,

Whose cries grew louder with each passing second.

Isaac swung the Plasma Cutter to expose the broken fuse,

Using his RIG once again to interface with the door pads.

But he was a second too late.

The man was already dead.

Feeling the vibrations of its scream,

He pointed the Plasma Cutter and fired,

Trying his best to aim at its limb,

it sounded easy on his head,

He failed to take account of its erratic movements.

His hands and feet began to feel cold.

He took a step back and continued firing.

Focusing his aim on its shoulders.

After multiple focused shots,

Its whole arm flicked away from the body.

Not letting go of the momentum,

he hacked its leg apart as well.

It fell like a log of lumber,

where it slobbered on the floor.

Eventually, he ran out of cartridges,

but the creature was still moving.

He walked right in front of it in desperation,

Letting out a guttural scream

as he stomped on it like an insect.

until the sole of his boot...

was riddled with flesh matter.

It was left with neither limbs nor a head,

just another carcass in a slaughterhouse.

But if cutting off the limbs only meant disabling it,

Does it truly fit the definition of being dead?
