• Part 4/4

Gaining back his senses,

he looked beneath his feet.

A monstrous limb,

a human head,

and a pool of blood.

The mechanical doors cut off parts of the creature as it closed.

Might’ve been a trick of the light or his mind,

But the thing seemed to have moved ever so slightly.

Not taking any more chances, he stepped into the room.

Where the muffled cries of a man can be heard.

First –look for anything he could use to defend himself.

His eyes darted frantically across the room in search of a weapon.

That’s when he caught a glimpse of it, screened by pipes,

After taking a short flight of stairs to the platform:

A gruesome scene enough to fuel nightmares.

In a seemingly normal workshop decorated with power tools--

Sits a mutilated man holding onto his spilled intestines.

A victim of the creatures, certain about his death.

With bloodied hands,

he approached the board and wrote:

CUT OFF THEIR LIMBS

Far from his calm demeanor,

he thought of ways to kill,

Isaac picks up one of the mining tools atop the workstation.
